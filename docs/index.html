About Our Game

Haste

This dynamic and hilarious game will make you get into the perspective of the salaryman.

This salaryman works for a japanese black company, his abusive boss told him to bring from Europe some food. There, he will get into countless troubles because of the shortage in those days. He will find that bad guys are causing problems all around Europe.
Cristopher Prostata and Dmitri Rasputin are making terrible things and you get involved to get the products you need

Would you be able to get in time and keep your job?
